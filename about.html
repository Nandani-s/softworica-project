    Your Future, Our Framework

    Empowering Nepal's Digital Future

    Softworica was founded with a vision to bridge the technology gap in Nepal and provide world-class IT solutions to businesses of all sizes. Based in Kawasoti, Nawalparasi, we have grown from a small startup to a trusted IT partner for numerous organizations across Nepal.


    Our journey began with a simple belief: that quality technology services should be accessible to everyone. Today, we serve clients ranging from startups to established enterprises, helping them navigate the digital landscape with innovative solutions and expert guidance.


    We don't just build software; we build relationships, empower businesses, and nurture the next generation of tech talent through our comprehensive training programs.

    Our Mission

    To deliver innovative, reliable, and cost-effective IT solutions that help businesses thrive in the digital age while fostering tech education and creating opportunities for aspiring developers in Nepal.

    Our Vision

    To be Nepal's most trusted IT solutions provider, recognized for excellence in software development, digital innovation, and tech education, contributing to the nation's digital transformation.
